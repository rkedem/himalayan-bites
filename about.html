About Us

Our Story

Himalayan Bites was created with a passion for sharing the flavors and traditions of the Himalayan region with the Queens community. What started as a simple idea grew into a place where people can gather, relax, and enjoy comforting food inspired by Nepal and the surrounding regions.

Our team believes that food is more than just a meal; it is a way to bring people together. Every dish is prepared with care, using fresh ingredients, vibrant spices, and recipes inspired by generations of home cooking.

Whether you are visiting for a casual lunch, a family dinner, or trying Himalayan cuisine for the first time, we hope every visit to Himalayan Bites feels welcoming, memorable, and full of flavor.
