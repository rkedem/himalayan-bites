A taste of the Himalayas

Discover Himalayan Bites, a Nepali restaurant in the heart of Queens. Since opening our doors in 2020, we have been dedicated to sharing the rich flavors and culinary traditions of the Himalayas in a warm and welcoming setting. Inspired by traditional recipes and vibrant spices, our kitchen brings together fresh ingredients and rich culinary traditions to create a memorable dining experience for every guest.
